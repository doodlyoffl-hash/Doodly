Profile & Attendance

The delivery executive's own account screen — read their driver record (zone, vehicle, rating, tenure) and keep their contact details and shift status current.

ℹ️
Note — where this screen lives

This page is served on the Delivery surface at /driver/profile.html (manifest route key driver/profile, surface: "driver"). It is the executive's self-service account page. The authoritative driver record — zone, vehicle number, delivery capacity, active/suspended state — is created and edited by an Admin on Admin → Operations → Drivers, and the day-by-day attendance ledger for salaried staff lives on Admin → Human Resources → Attendance. Both are cross-referenced below.

Best Practice

Ask each executive to confirm both numbers on their first login and after any SIM change. Keep the emergency contact different from the primary phone (a family member or nearest hub), so ops can reach someone if the executive is unreachable on the road.

Shift & Attendance

Start / end shift availability toggle

Purpose The executive marks themselves on- or off-shift. Only executives who are on-shift (AVAILABLE) receive new auto-assigned deliveries, so this toggle is the real attendance signal for the routing engine.
Access Delivery Executive (own shift) · Super Admin (oversight) Path Delivery → Today → Dashboard (availability pill)

The shift control appears on the executive's route dashboard as a tappable status pill in the header — a green "🟢 Available for assignment" when on-shift, or a grey "⚪ Not available — start shift" when off. It is driven by the executive's own availability endpoint and is the closest thing to a clock-in for a delivery executive: it decides whether the auto-assignment engine can hand them work.

Availability states

The executive's live status is stored on their ExecutiveStatus row. The engine treats only AVAILABLE (and RETURNED_TO_DAIRY, i.e. back at the hub, empty) as eligible for new work.

ℹ️
Every shift change is audited

Each toggle writes an AssignmentLog entry with action STATUS_CHANGE, the executive as the actor (actorRole: delivery_executive) and a note such as "shift started → AVAILABLE" or "shift ended → OFFLINE". A Super Admin can review this trail in the assignment history — it is the auditable record of when each executive clocked on and off.

⚠️
Warning — you cannot go offline mid-trip

If the executive is ASSIGNED, ACCEPTED or OUT_FOR_DELIVERY, trying to end their shift is rejected with a 409 and the message: "You have an active trip — complete your deliveries (or mark returned to dairy) before changing availability." They must finish the round (or mark returned to dairy) first. This protects customers from a half-delivered route being abandoned.

💡
Tip — the missing-profile guard

Both the availability endpoint and the day-summary endpoint require a driver profile linked to the account. If someone signs in without one, they get "No delivery profile is linked to this account." Fix it by creating the driver on Admin → Operations → Drivers and linking it to their user.

Attendance ledger (HR)

Where a salaried executive's days are recorded

Purpose For executives who are on payroll, formal day-by-day attendance (present, leave, off) is recorded in Human Resources and feeds payroll — separate from the live shift toggle above.
Access Super Admin · Admin · Manager (HR-permitted) Path Admin → Human Resources → Attendance

The shift toggle answers "are they working right now?"; the HR attendance ledger answers "how many days did they work this month?" for pay. Each attendance record (Attendance) carries a date, a status, optional check-in / check-out times, worked minutes, overtime minutes and a source (manual, biometric, etc.). It attaches to an EmployeeProfile, so it applies to any delivery executive who is also set up as an employee.

ℹ️
Note — two separate ideas, on purpose

The live availability pill and the HR attendance ledger are intentionally distinct: the pill drives routing in real time, the ledger drives payroll. Full documentation of the attendance ledger, bulk marking and payroll linkage lives in the Human Resources chapter (Attendance module).

Frequently asked questions

Can an executive change their own zone, vehicle or rating on this page?

No. Those are read-only on the Profile card. Zone and vehicle are set by an Admin on the Drivers screen; rating is averaged automatically from delivery feedback. Only Phone and Emergency contact are editable by the executive.

Does ending a shift cancel deliveries already assigned?

No. You cannot end a shift while a trip is active — the system blocks it (409). You must complete or return the current trip first, which is exactly why abandoned half-routes cannot happen from the toggle.

Is the shift toggle the same as marking attendance for payroll?

No. The toggle is a live routing signal (AVAILABLE vs OFFLINE) and is audited in the assignment log. Payroll attendance is recorded separately in Admin → Human Resources → Attendance against the executive's employee profile.

Where can a Super Admin see when an executive clocked on and off?

In the assignment audit trail: every shift change writes an AssignmentLog row of type STATUS_CHANGE with the timestamp, the executive as actor, and a note like "shift started → AVAILABLE".
