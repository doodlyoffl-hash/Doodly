Cash Collection

A read-only, self-service view that totals the cash-on-delivery (COD) a delivery executive has collected on today's route, so they can reconcile and hand it in at the hub.

ℹ️
Note — whose screen this is

Cash Collection lives in the driver portal, not the admin console. It shows one executive their own numbers only. The data endpoint refuses anyone who does not have a Driver profile linked to their account ("No delivery profile is linked to this account."). A Super Admin reads this section to understand the driver's daily cash workflow and to oversee reconciliation from Admin → Operations → Delivery Mgmt; they do not personally use this screen.

💡
Tip — why the total may exceed the visible rows' sum on a busy day

The Cash collected today stat sums cash across all of today's stops, while the table lists only stops with cash. In normal operation these agree, because a stop only has cash after it is delivered. If you ever see a mismatch, refresh — a stop was likely just updated on the Route screen.

⛔
Caution — cash can't be edited from the driver app once delivered

After a stop is marked DELIVERED, the stop card switches to a read-only summary ("✓ Delivered … · ₹70 cash") and the cash field disappears. The executive cannot re-open it to correct a wrong amount. A mistake must be fixed by an Admin/Super Admin on the delivery record. Enter the exact amount before tapping Confirm delivered.

✅
Best Practice — daily hub reconciliation

At the end of every route, open Cash Collection, read the Cash collected today total, and count the physical notes to match it before you hand cash to the hub. Because most orders are prepaid, the Cash payments count should equal the number of COD stops on your route — if it is lower, a COD stop was delivered with the cash field left blank; report it so Admin can correct the record.

⚠️
Warning — blank cash field defaults to ₹0

Leaving the Cash collected field empty on a COD stop records ₹0, not the amount owed. The order is not held or flagged for this. Always type the amount on cash orders; only leave it blank for genuinely prepaid deliveries.

Frequently asked questions

Q: Do I type cash amounts on the Cash Collection screen?

No. Cash Collection is read-only. You enter cash on the Route screen in the "Mark delivered" form; it then rolls up here automatically.

Q: Why is the amount stored in paise?

All money in DOODLY is stored as integer paise to avoid rounding errors. The rupees you type are multiplied by 100 on save, and the screen divides back and rounds to whole rupees for display.

Q: Where does the cash physically go?

You hand collected cash to the hub at the end of the route. The app's job is reconciliation — matching your counted cash to the recorded total against delivered COD stops.

Q: Can a Super Admin see a driver's cash?

Not on this driver-only screen. Super Admin oversight of delivered stops, cash and reconciliation is done through the admin Delivery Management module, which reads the same Delivery records.
